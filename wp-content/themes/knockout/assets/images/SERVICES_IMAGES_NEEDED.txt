REQUIRED IMAGES FOR SERVICES SECTION
=====================================

Please add the following images to the assets/images/ folder:

1. lavish-food.jpg - High-quality image of your food/restaurant area
2. pubs-bars.jpg - Image of your bar/pub area with drinks/atmosphere  
3. hookah-lounge.jpg - Image of your hookah lounge area
4. bowling-lanes.jpg - Image of your 6-lane bowling alley
5. arcade-games.jpg - Image of your AR/VR gaming zone and arcade games

Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600 pixels minimum
- High quality/resolution for best display
- Should showcase the actual areas of your establishment

Once you add these images, your services section will display perfectly with the card-based layout matching your design requirements.

Note: The services template (template-parts/services.php) and CSS (assets/css/experience-section.css) are already configured and ready to use.
